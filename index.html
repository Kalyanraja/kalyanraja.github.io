RESEARCH WORK

My areas of interest are Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Computer Vision.

Tabular Embeddings for Tables with Bi-Dimensional Hierarchical Metadata and Nesting

Florida State University
28th International Conference on Extending Database Technology, 2025

This research presents novel embeddings designed for encoding complex 2D tabular data with hierarchical metadata and nesting. We introduce bi-dimensional tabular coordinates and a visibility matrix to capture horizontal and vertical structures efficiently. Evaluations on five large datasets and three downstream tasks show a significant improvement in MAP scores (up to 0.28). While GPT-4 LLM+RAG slightly outperforms our method in MRR, our approach achieves a MAP delta of up to 0.42.

CancerKG - A Web-scale, Interactive, Verifiable Knowledge Graph-LLM Hybrid for Optimal Cancer Treatment and Care

Florida State University
Proceedings of the 33rd ACM International Conference on Information and Knowledge Management, 2023

CancerKG is one of the first Web-scale hybrid Knowledge Graph (KG)-Large Language Model (LLM) systems populated with the latest peer-reviewed medical knowledge on colorectal cancer. It is currently being evaluated for assisting medical research and clinical tasks at Moffitt Cancer Center. This system mitigates LLM limitations such as hallucinations and outdated corpora by integrating a verified KG for Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG). It features five advanced user interfaces optimized for different medical data modalities, achieving a high NDCG score on a dataset of approximately 44K medical publications.

Optimizers in Deep Learning: A Comparative Study and Analysis

Sujay Bashetty, Kalyan Raja, Sahiti Adepu, Ajeet Jain
International Journal for Research in Applied Science & Engineering Technology (IJRASET), 2021

This research provides an empirical comparison of deep learning optimization algorithms, including SGD, Adam, and RMSProp, on MNIST and Auto Encoder datasets. The study analyzes training speeds, convergence rates, and final accuracy levels, highlighting key trade-offs between optimizers and their suitability for various neural network architectures.

Driver Safety Prediction System

Kalyan Raja K, Sahiti Adepu
International Journal of Science and Research (IJSR), 2020

This paper introduces a real-time driver safety prediction system using CNNs and OpenCV. It detects driver drowsiness and recognizes traffic signs to prevent accidents. The system integrates eye aspect ratio-based drowsiness detection and real-time traffic sign recognition to enhance driving safety.

Cardiovascular Disease Prediction using Genetic Algorithm

Bhavya Pandya, Naman Mistry, Sujendra Gharat, Kalyan Raja Kadari, Sahiti Adepu
International Journal of All Research Education and Scientific Methods (IJARESM), ISSN: 2455-6211, 2020

Cardiovascular diseases are long-term health conditions affecting the heart, often requiring years to diagnose and treat. Early and accurate detection is crucial, as errors in diagnosis can be life-threatening. This research introduces a cost-effective prediction system for cardiovascular diseases using Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) and binary classification. It leverages common risk factors such as age, family history, diabetes, hypertension, high cholesterol, smoking, alcohol intake, obesity, and physical inactivity. Unlike traditional methods, this system can provide early warnings based on these risk indicators before a patient even visits a hospital. By analyzing large datasets, it enables early-stage risk assessment, helping both medical professionals and patients take preventive action before critical symptoms appear.
